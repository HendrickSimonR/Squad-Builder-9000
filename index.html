How It Works

Head-to-Head Points

This app is structured around points, where a win is achieved by totaling more fantasy points against an opponent. Typically, values for a statistic are evaluated by these metrics:

  • Points (+1)
  • Rebounds (+1.2)
  • Assists (+1.5)
  • Steals/Blocks (+3)
  • Turnovers (-1)

    • The Squad-Builder 9000 algorithm works as follows:

    offensivePoints = Points + (Assists * 1.5)

    defensivePoints = (Rebounds * 1.2) + (Steals * 3) + (Blocks * 3)

    Player Fantasy Average = offensivePoints + defensivePoints - Turnovers

    "Snake-Ordered" Draft

    A snake-ordered draft implies that once a round of drafts ends, the next round begins at the end, starting with who went last in the previous round until it reaches who originally went first. The following round will start again with who went last prior, and will continue this way until drafting is complete.

    Pro-Tip

    Try to play around with different combinations (with and without a preferred draft pick) to see how the line-ups change and the starting line-up fantasy average changes!
