BUILD YOUR SQUAD!

Or rather, let the algorithm build it for you! Using the average player stats for the current NBA season, this app will present your best* possible lineup in a snake-ordered fantasy draft.

Instructions

  • Provide a favorite player (optional).
  • Pick how many participants will be part of your draft.
  • Select your placement in the draft.
  • Reveal your squad!

    • * It's your call who the best is! This app is powered by the BallDontLie API.
